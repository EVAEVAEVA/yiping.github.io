Dramafest 2012: Crayons

The looking back upon childhood.

As the first design project I ever did since the entrance to university, the entire journey throughout was new to me.

The entire process, ranging from brainstorming, idealisation, sketching, asking for help from my seniors to take the background image, till making of the poster, as well as limitless iterations was mainly on my own.
The most important thing that I have learned through this process is not the stack of PS skills. More importantly, the thinking process before getting hands dirty; the extension of self-originated ideas that would actually define the design; your desire of how the design would become.
With these in mind, the design process thereafter has been smoother and I could remain calm even in face of lack of inspirations or technical difficulties.

Milestone Value
“Skills could always be learned.
The process of thinking behind the design and how you want it to become is more important.”
