The first ever official UX Designer position.

The internship was a fun experience. I worked with a team of people from across the globe, and mainly responsible for converting the web product into the UX for its iOS application.
There was a significant amount of knowledge obtained throughout the process. Again, the process stared with market research, competitor study, idealisation and iteration, sketching for ideas, group discussion, settle on the MVP, design the wireframe for key pages, construct wireframes and flowmap for the entire application till designing the simple styleguide.
The project passed on to software developers and a UI designer, while I had to focus on my final year project. It was a great experience.

Milestone Value
“The value and important factor in design is way beyond aesthetics and usability.
Most of the time, we would have to consider what are the current achievables, what are the things must-to-do instead of good-to-have, and what are the thing we had to left over with.”
