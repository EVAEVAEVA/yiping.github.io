The process

How it all started

Since 2021, a cross-functional team in Janssen APAC region started to build a future-looking data & business insight platform. The vision, is for all business functions to have an all-in-one platform to find relevant insight for any diseases.
The goal is to uphold the competitive edge by enabling faster and more efficient decisions making & strategic plannings.
The team have considered the technological infrastructure, data ingestion, platform development.. but wait, there's a missing link to the end users. How can we make this platform intuitive, excitable and useful for people?
This was when my team was involved.

"Intuitive and meaningful data & business insight"

The full team

Before JJD (J&J Design) was involved, there're already hundred plus people on the project team. The core team members are primarily from Janssen business, technology and data teams, KPMG who helped to shape the strategic directions, as well as Clarivate business, technology and data teams to support the platform build and integrations.
The primary focused business functions include: Commercial, Market Access, Medical Affairs, R&D, Real-world data & evidence, New Business Development, Therapeutic area & Disease area leads, Business Intelligence, etc.
The approach was to devise th 80% common usecases across ALL business functions and use it as the commonground to build the foundation features of the platform.

The ask

Our business partner has reached out to us primarily due to the lack of WOW factor on the MVP platform. Built on Tableau off-the-shelf libraries and with a quick turnaround time, there were nothing sexy about the platform except for the powerful data insights and bright vision.
