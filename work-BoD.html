How it all started

Since 2021, a cross-functional regional team in Janssen started to build a future-looking data & business insight platform; the vision is for this to be the all-in-one platform, where people from any business function can find relevant insight for any diseases.
The goal is to have the competitive edge by enabling faster and more efficient business decisions making & strategic plannings.
The team have considered infrastructure, data ingestion, platform technological development.. but wait, there's a missing link to the end users. How do we make this platform intuitive, excitable and useful for people?
This was when my team was involved.
