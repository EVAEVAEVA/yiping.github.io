Shoppin: The Smart Shopping List

Never forget the shopping need of you and your loved ones.

The project was the final project for a module entitled "CS3216: Software Development for Evolving Platforms". The module opened the gateway to my experiences of UI design for applications.

The module started many "first time doing something." We needed to produce a storyboard, poster demonstration as well as application prototype for this project. As the designer on team, it was a great responsibility of how to present and convey our product idea to the other people.
Not only was it the first time did I produced a video from keynote, it also marks the starting journey of my UI/UX design experiences, the first time when I begin to use HTML/CSS seriously, and even more important is that Dr. Collin let the entire group, especially me, realize that the group's effort, despite the hard work of all software developers, is closely related and linked with the designer.
And I never stopped self-initiative learning since.
